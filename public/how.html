how it works

It’s simple! Sign up for FriendLeaf by providing your first and last name, email, and age. Then, answer a short survey of 12 questions designed to foster connection and interaction.

If you choose “Match Me Now!” we’ll connect you with your FriendLeaf Match — just check your email for the results. If you choose “Match Me Later!” we’ll add you to our FriendLeaf database and notify you when a match has been made. Happy connecting!
