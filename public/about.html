about

FriendLeaf is developed by a team of four multi-talented students known as “Dark Mode Superiority.” Team DMS is passionate about front-end development, programming, and design.


Concerned about increasing feelings of isolation during the COVID-19 pandemic, they were inspired to create an app that could bring people together. FriendLeaf prides itself on being easy to use and effective. Contact us with any questions or comments regarding your experience and how we can improve FriendLeaf. Happy connecting!
